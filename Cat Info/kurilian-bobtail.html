Kurilian Bobtail

Personalities: Playful, Loyal, Curious

The Kurilian Bobtail is a unique and lively breed recognized for its distinctive bobbed tail and playful demeanor. Originating from the Kuril Islands, these cats are highly intelligent, loyal, and known for their strong bond with their families. Kurilian Bobtails are also curious and energetic, making them excellent companions for active households. Their short tails and muscular build give them a robust appearance, and they are often described as "dog-like" because of their loyalty and playful nature. They enjoy interacting with humans and other pets and are known to be good with children.

Did You Know? The Kurilian Bobtail's unique bobbed tail is a naturally occurring mutation and is a distinct feature of the breed.

Favorite Food: Fish and poultry

Age: Typically 12-15 years
