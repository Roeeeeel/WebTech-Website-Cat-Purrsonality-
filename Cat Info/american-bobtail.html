American Bobtail Cat

Personalities: Friendly, Playful, Adaptable

The American Bobtail is a loving and playful breed known for its distinctive bobbed tail. They are highly adaptable and get along well with children, other pets, and even strangers. Their intelligence and dog-like personality make them a unique companion.

Did You Know? American Bobtails are often called "golden retrievers of the cat world" due to their friendly and sociable nature.

Favorite Food: Tuna and turkey

Age: Typically 13-16 years

Breed: American Bobtail
