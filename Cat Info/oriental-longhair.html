Oriental Longhair

Personalities: Playful, Affectionate, Intelligent

The Oriental Longhair is an elegant and graceful breed with a long, silky coat and striking almond-shaped eyes. Much like its short-haired counterpart, the Oriental Shorthair, the Oriental Longhair is known for its playful, intelligent, and affectionate nature. They are social cats who love to be around people and are often curious about their surroundings. These cats enjoy interactive play and mental stimulation, and they thrive in environments where they can be part of family activities.

Did You Know? The Oriental Longhair is a variation of the Oriental Shorthair, and it was developed to maintain the breed's lively personality while showcasing a longer coat.

Favorite Food: Chicken and turkey

Age: Typically 12-15 years
