Nebelung

Personalities: Gentle, Playful, Loyal

The Nebelung is an elegant and striking breed known for its long, shimmering blue-gray coat and green eyes. These cats are gentle, affectionate, and loyal companions, often bonding closely with their families. While they are playful and curious, Nebelungs are also known for being reserved and may take time to warm up to strangers. They are highly intelligent and enjoy interactive play, making them a great choice for families who want a calm yet engaging cat.

Did You Know? The Nebelung's name is derived from the German word "nebel," meaning "fog," which refers to the breed's misty coat color.

Favorite Food: Chicken and turkey

Age: Typically 12-16 years
