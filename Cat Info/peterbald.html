Peterbald

Personalities: Affectionate, Energetic, Social

The Peterbald is an elegant, sleek breed known for its unique hairless or short-haired coat. This breed is affectionate, energetic, and highly social. Peterbalds are known for being playful and curious, often following their owners around the house. They thrive on human interaction and love being part of the family. Despite their striking appearance, Peterbalds are gentle and loving cats who are often described as "dog-like" due to their loyal nature and need for attention.

Did You Know? The Peterbald breed was developed in Russia in the early 1990s and is a cross between the Don Sphynx and Oriental Shorthair breeds.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-16 years
