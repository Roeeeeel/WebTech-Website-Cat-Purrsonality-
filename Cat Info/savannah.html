Savannah

Personalities: Active, Adventurous, Intelligent

The Savannah cat is known for its wild appearance, resembling a small wild serval, with large ears and a long neck. They are highly energetic, inquisitive, and social. These cats require ample space to roam and thrive in active environments. They are also known for being extremely loyal to their owners and are good with other pets and children.

Did You Know? The Savannah cat is one of the largest domestic cats, with some individuals weighing up to 25 pounds.

Favorite Food: Chicken, fish, and high-protein meals

Age: Typically 12-20 years

Breed: Savannah
