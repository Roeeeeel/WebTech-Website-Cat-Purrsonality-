Selkirk Rex

Personalities: Affectionate, Playful, Adaptable

The Selkirk Rex is known for its curly fur, which is a result of a natural mutation. These cats have a sweet, laid-back personality, making them excellent companions. They are highly affectionate and social, enjoying human interaction and getting along well with children and other pets. Their playful nature makes them a joy to have around, and they adapt well to various living situations.

Did You Know? The Selkirk Rex's curly coat is easy to maintain and requires only occasional brushing.

Favorite Food: Chicken, beef, and fish

Age: Typically 12-16 years
