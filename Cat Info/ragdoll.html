Ragdoll

Personalities: Gentle, Affectionate, Calm

The Ragdoll is a large, gentle breed known for its relaxed temperament and affectionate nature. They are often described as "puppy-like" due to their love for following their owners around and being held. Their striking blue eyes and silky, semi-long coat add to their charm. Ragdolls are highly social cats and tend to get along well with other pets and children, making them ideal family companions.

Did You Know? The Ragdoll breed was developed in the 1960s in California and is famous for going limp when picked up, hence the name "Ragdoll."

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-15 years
