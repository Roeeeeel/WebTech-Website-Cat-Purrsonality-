Scottish Fold

Personalities: Calm, Affectionate, Playful

The Scottish Fold is most recognized for its unique folded ears, which give the breed a distinct and adorable appearance. These cats are friendly, affectionate, and tend to be quite calm. Scottish Folds enjoy companionship and make excellent pets for families, as they are typically good with children and other animals.
