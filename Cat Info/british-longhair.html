British Longhair

Personalities: Calm, Affectionate, Independent

The British Longhair is a luxurious, medium-to-long-haired breed known for its round face, large eyes, and dense coat. With their sweet nature and affectionate disposition, they are ideal companions for families, but they also enjoy their independence. These cats are known for being easy-going, loving, and generally good with children and other pets.

Did You Know? The British Longhair was originally the result of crossbreeding the British Shorthair with longhaired cats, and it is often recognized for its plush, teddy-bear-like appearance.

Favorite Food: Fish and beef

Age: Typically 12-16 years
