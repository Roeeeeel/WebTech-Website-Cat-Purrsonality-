Himalayan

Personalities: Calm, Affectionate, Gentle

The Himalayan is a stunningly beautiful breed known for its luxurious long coat and striking blue eyes. A cross between the Persian and Siamese breeds, the Himalayan has inherited the best of both worlds— a calm, affectionate nature and a stunning appearance. They are typically quiet and gentle, making them ideal companions for people who enjoy a laid-back, loving pet. Himalayans are highly affectionate, and they form strong bonds with their families. Despite their calm demeanor, they enjoy playful moments and appreciate interactive playtime.

Did You Know? The Himalayan is also known as the “Colorpoint Persian” due to its color-pointed fur, which resembles the Siamese cat, but with the long, luxurious coat of a Persian.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-15 years
