Tonkinese

Personalities: Playful, Affectionate, Social

The Tonkinese is a lively and playful breed with a unique coat that combines the best traits of both the Siamese and Burmese. Known for their striking blue or aqua eyes and sleek, muscular body, Tonkinese cats are highly affectionate and enjoy being around their human companions. They are social cats that thrive in homes with plenty of interaction and playtime. Their playful nature makes them great companions for families and other pets. They are intelligent, curious, and can quickly learn tricks or games, and their vocalizations are often soft and sweet.

Did You Know? The Tonkinese is a relatively new breed, first developed in the 1960s by crossing Siamese and Burmese cats to combine the best features of both breeds.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-16 years
