Japanese Bobtail

Personalities: Playful, Energetic, Affectionate

The Japanese Bobtail is a lively and friendly breed known for its unique bobbed tail and energetic nature. These cats are playful, affectionate, and highly social, making them great companions for active families. They are highly intelligent and curious, enjoying interactive play and problem-solving activities. Japanese Bobtails are also known for their strong bonds with their human companions and will often follow their owners around the house. Their distinct bobtail, which resembles a pom-pom, and their joyful disposition make them easily recognizable and adored.

Did You Know? The Japanese Bobtail is considered a symbol of good luck in Japan and is often depicted in traditional art and folklore.

Favorite Food: Fish and chicken

Age: Typically 12-16 years

Breed: Japanese Bobtail
