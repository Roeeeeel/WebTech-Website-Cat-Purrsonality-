Toyger

Personalities: Playful, Intelligent, Affectionate

The Toyger is a striking breed known for its wild appearance, resembling a miniature tiger with its beautiful, striped coat. Despite its fierce looks, the Toyger is a friendly, affectionate, and playful cat. They are known for their intelligence and curiosity, often engaging in interactive play and problem-solving activities. Toygers enjoy being around people and can be quite social. They have a love for climbing and exploring, so providing them with vertical spaces is important. This breed is also quite vocal, often communicating with its owners in a friendly manner.

Did You Know? The Toyger was specifically bred to resemble a tiger, using domestic cats and wild-looking breeds such as the Bengal to create its unique appearance.

Favorite Food: Chicken and tuna

Age: Typically 12-15 years
