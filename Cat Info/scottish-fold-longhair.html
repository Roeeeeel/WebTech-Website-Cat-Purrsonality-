Scottish Fold Longhair

Personalities: Calm, Affectionate, Playful

The Scottish Fold Longhair is a stunning variation of the Scottish Fold, featuring long, luxurious fur and those signature folded ears. These cats are just as affectionate and playful as their shorthaired counterparts, and they enjoy being around their family members. They are gentle and sweet, making them perfect companions for households with children and other pets.

Did You Know? Scottish Fold Longhairs need regular grooming to keep their long coats in tip-top shape.

Favorite Food: Chicken, turkey, and fish

Age: Typically 11-15 years

Breed: Scottish Fold Longhair
