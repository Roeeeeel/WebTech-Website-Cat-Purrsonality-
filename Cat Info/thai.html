Thai

Personalities: Friendly, Social, Affectionate

The Thai cat is a native breed from Thailand, often referred to as the "Wichienmaat." These cats have a sleek, elegant appearance with almond-shaped eyes and a short coat that comes in various colors. Thai cats are known for their affectionate and social nature. They are people-oriented, forming strong bonds with their families and enjoying companionship. Playful and curious, Thai cats are also highly intelligent and can be quite vocal when they want attention. They are generally friendly with other pets and adapt well to different living environments.

Did You Know? The Thai breed is believed to be one of the ancestors of the modern Siamese cat, and its history dates back over a thousand years in Thailand.

Favorite Food: Fish and poultry

Age: Typically 12-16 years
