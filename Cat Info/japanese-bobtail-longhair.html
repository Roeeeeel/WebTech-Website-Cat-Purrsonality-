Japanese Bobtail Longhair

Personalities: Playful, Energetic, Affectionate

The Japanese Bobtail Longhair is a charming and elegant breed known for its distinctive bobtail and flowing coat. This breed is playful, energetic, and highly affectionate, making them an ideal companion for active families. They are known for their intelligence and curiosity, often engaging in interactive play and problem-solving activities. Japanese Bobtails are particularly social, and they enjoy spending time with their human companions, often following them around the house. Their long, silky fur and bobtail, which is said to resemble a pom-pom, make them easily recognizable and adored.

Did You Know? The Japanese Bobtail is a symbol of good luck in Japan, and is often depicted in traditional artwork and the famous "Maneki-neko" or "beckoning cat" statues.

Favorite Food: Fish and chicken

Age: Typically 12-16 years
