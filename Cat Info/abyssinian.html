Abyssinian Cat

Personalities: Energetic, Intelligent, Loyal

The Abyssinian cat is one of the oldest known breeds, admired for its ticked coat and lively nature. These cats are incredibly curious and love to explore their surroundings. They enjoy climbing and being part of family activities.

Did You Know? Abyssinians are sometimes called "Aby-grabbers" because they love to swipe at objects out of curiosity and playfulness.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-15 years

Breed: Abyssinian
