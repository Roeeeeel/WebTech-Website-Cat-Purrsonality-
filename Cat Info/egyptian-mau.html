Egyptian Mau

Personalities: Loyal, Playful, Graceful

The Egyptian Mau is a rare and elegant breed known for its spotted coat and striking green eyes. As one of the fastest domestic cat breeds, the Mau is athletic, graceful, and loves to play. These cats are also very loyal and form strong bonds with their families, often following their humans around the house. With a natural affinity for hunting, the Egyptian Mau is both active and affectionate, making it a great companion for families and individuals alike.

Did You Know? The Egyptian Mau is one of the few naturally spotted breeds, and its spots are unique to each cat, much like fingerprints.

Favorite Food: Poultry and fish

Age: Typically 12-15 years
