Cornish Rex

Personalities: Energetic, Affectionate, Social

The Cornish Rex is a unique and playful breed known for its short, wavy coat and elegant body. With its distinctive appearance, including large ears and high cheekbones, the Cornish Rex is a true standout. These cats are incredibly social and thrive on human interaction, making them perfect companions for families and individuals alike. They are energetic, curious, and love to play, often displaying kitten-like behavior well into adulthood.

Did You Know? The Cornish Rex's coat lacks guard hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture unlike any other breed.

Favorite Food: Chicken and tuna

Age: Typically 12-15 years

Breed: Cornish Rex
