Tennessee Rex

Personalities: Friendly, Affectionate, Active

The Tennessee Rex is a unique and rare breed characterized by its curly coat, which is soft and plush. Known for their lively and affectionate nature, these cats are friendly, playful, and enjoy being the center of attention. They are very social and love interacting with their human companions, often following them around the house. Tennessee Rex cats are also known for their intelligence and curiosity, always looking for new activities and things to explore. Despite their energy, they are gentle and make excellent companions for families and individuals alike.

Did You Know? The Tennessee Rex is a relatively new breed, first discovered in the 1980s in Tennessee, USA, and it was named after the state where it originated.

Favorite Food: Chicken and tuna

Age: Typically 12-15 years
