Minuet

Personalities: Playful, Affectionate, Friendly

The Minuet is a delightful and compact breed, known for its short legs, round face, and sweet temperament. These cats are incredibly playful and affectionate, often forming strong bonds with their human companions. Minuets are social cats, loving to be around people and enjoy interactive play. Their gentle nature makes them great companions for families with children or other pets. With their unique looks and friendly disposition, Minuets are sure to bring joy to any household.

Did You Know? The Minuet breed was developed by crossing the Munchkin cat with Persian and Exotic Shorthair breeds, resulting in a cat with a short stature and a lovable personality.

Favorite Food: Chicken and tuna

Age: Typically 12-15 years
