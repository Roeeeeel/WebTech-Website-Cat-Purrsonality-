Chartreux

Personalities: Quiet, Intelligent, Affectionate

The Chartreux is a beautiful French breed known for its dense, blue-gray coat and vibrant orange eyes. This cat is often described as being a quiet companion, with a calm and gentle demeanor. While they may not be as vocal as other breeds, Chartreux cats are deeply affectionate and form strong bonds with their families. They are also highly intelligent and playful, making them excellent companions for people of all ages.

Did You Know? The Chartreux is one of the oldest natural cat breeds and has been associated with French monasteries, where they were believed to have been bred by monks.

Favorite Food: Tuna and lamb

Age: Typically 12-15 years
