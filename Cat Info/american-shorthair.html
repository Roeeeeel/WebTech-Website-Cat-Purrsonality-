American Shorthair

Personalities: Friendly, Adaptable, Easygoing

The American Shorthair is a beloved breed known for its well-balanced temperament and versatility. With a history of being skilled hunters, these cats are strong and agile while maintaining a calm and friendly nature. They make excellent pets for families, including those with children or other animals.

Did You Know? American Shorthairs were originally brought to North America on ships to protect cargo from rodents.

Favorite Food: Beef and fish

Age: Typically 15-20 years
