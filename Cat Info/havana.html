Havana Brown

Personalities: Affectionate, Playful, Intelligent

The Havana Brown is an elegant and graceful breed, known for its deep, chocolate-colored coat and striking green eyes. These cats are friendly, affectionate, and playful, often bonding closely with their human companions. Despite their regal appearance, they are active and curious, enjoying interactive play and exploration. Havana Browns are known for their intelligence and problem-solving abilities, making them a joy to engage with. They tend to be very social and are known for being "dog-like" in their loyalty to their families.

Did You Know? The Havana Brown is a rare breed, and its rich chocolate coat is the result of a unique genetic combination, giving it its distinctive color.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-15 years
