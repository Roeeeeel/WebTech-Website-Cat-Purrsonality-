Siamese

Personalities: Vocal, Social, Intelligent

The Siamese is one of the most recognizable and beloved cat breeds, known for its sleek body, striking blue eyes, and pointed coat pattern. These cats are highly social, intelligent, and affectionate, often forming strong bonds with their humans. Siamese cats are also famously vocal, frequently "talking" to their owners in their unique, loud voices. They thrive in interactive and stimulating environments where they can be the center of attention.

Did You Know? The Siamese is one of the oldest and most revered cat breeds, originating in Thailand (formerly Siam) and often featured in ancient art and literature.

Favorite Food: Fish and poultry

Age: Typically 12-15 years

Breed: Siamese
