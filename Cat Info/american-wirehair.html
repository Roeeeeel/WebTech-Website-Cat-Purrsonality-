American Wirehair

Personalities: Playful, Affectionate, Independent

The American Wirehair is a unique breed distinguished by its coarse, springy coat. These cats are playful and affectionate but also enjoy their independence. They are adaptable and make great companions for both families and individuals.

Did You Know? The American Wirehair's unusual coat is the result of a natural genetic mutation, and no two coats are exactly alike!

Favorite Food: Chicken and lamb

Age: Typically 14-18 years
