Pixiebob Longhair

Personalities: Playful, Loyal, Intelligent

The Pixiebob Longhair is a striking breed with a wild appearance resembling a bobcat. They are known for their tufted ears, bobbed tails, and distinctive coat patterns. Despite their wild look, they are friendly, playful, and extremely affectionate. Pixiebobs are often very attached to their families and enjoy interactive play and exploring. Their intelligence makes them easy to train, and they get along well with children and other pets.

Did You Know? The Pixiebob breed was originally created in the 1980s, and although they look like wild bobcats, they are a domesticated breed with a gentle temperament.

Favorite Food: Chicken and turkey

Age: Typically 13-16 years
