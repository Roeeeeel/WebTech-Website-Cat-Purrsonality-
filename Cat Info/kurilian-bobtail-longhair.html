Kurilian Bobtail Longhair

Personalities: Playful, Loyal, Intelligent

The Kurilian Bobtail Longhair is a unique and charming breed known for its short, bobbed tail and long, luxurious coat. Originating from the Kuril Islands, this breed is playful, highly intelligent, and known for its strong bond with its family. The Kurilian Bobtail Longhair is a social and active cat, often described as "dog-like" due to its loyalty and affectionate nature. They enjoy interacting with humans and other pets, and their curious minds make them quick learners. Despite their playful demeanor, they also appreciate calm moments of affection and grooming, which helps maintain their beautiful long coat.

Did You Know? The Kurilian Bobtail Longhair is believed to have been brought to the Kuril Islands by sailors, and its bobtail is a distinctive trait that evolved naturally over time.

Favorite Food: Fish and chicken

Age: Typically 12-15 years
