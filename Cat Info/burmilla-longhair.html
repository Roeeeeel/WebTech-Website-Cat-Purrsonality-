Burmilla Longhair

Personalities: Gentle, Affectionate, Playful

The Burmilla Longhair is a rare and stunning breed with a silky semi-long coat and a soft, flowing tail. As a variation of the Burmilla, it shares the same friendly and affectionate nature but is distinguished by its luxurious fur. These cats are social, playful, and thrive in family environments where they can interact with their loved ones. Their elegant appearance is complemented by their charming and gentle personality.

Did You Know? The Burmilla Longhair is a result of a recessive gene in the Burmilla breed, giving it its striking semi-long coat.

Favorite Food: Chicken and turkey

Age: Typically 12-16 years
