Selkirk Rex Longhair

Personalities: Affectionate, Playful, Adaptable

The Selkirk Rex Longhair is a beautiful variation of the Selkirk Rex, known for its long, curly coat. These cats are friendly, gentle, and playful, with a strong desire for human companionship. They adapt easily to new environments and enjoy spending time with children and other pets. Their distinctive curls and luxurious long fur make them stand out in any crowd.

Did You Know? Like their shorthaired cousins, the Selkirk Rex Longhair's curly coat only requires occasional grooming to maintain its softness.

Favorite Food: Salmon, chicken, and tuna

Age: Typically 12-16 years
