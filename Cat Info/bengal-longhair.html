Bengal Longhair

Personalities: Active, Curious, Affectionate

The Bengal Longhair, also known as the Cashmere Bengal, is a rare and gorgeous variation of the Bengal breed. With their silky, semi-long coat and wild-looking markings, they are as striking as they are lovable. These cats are just as energetic, playful, and intelligent as their shorthaired relatives, and they thrive in interactive and stimulating environments.

Did You Know? Bengal Longhairs are not a separate breed but rather a longhaired variation of the Bengal, resulting from a recessive gene.

Favorite Food: Salmon and turkey

Age: Typically 12-16 years

Breed: Bengal Longhair
