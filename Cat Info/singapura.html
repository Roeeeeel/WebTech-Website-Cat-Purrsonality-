Singapura

Personalities: Playful, Affectionate, Curious

The Singapura is a small yet lively breed, known for its affectionate nature and stunning, ticked coat. Despite their small size, these cats have big personalities. Singapura cats are incredibly playful and love to explore their surroundings. They are also known for being curious, often getting into mischief as they investigate every corner of the house. Affectionate and social, they bond closely with their human companions and are particularly known for being vocal and communicative. Their compact, muscular body and large eyes make them both endearing and elegant in appearance.

Did You Know? The Singapura is one of the smallest cat breeds in the world and was first developed in Singapore before being recognized internationally.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-15 years
