Maine Coon

Personalities: Friendly, Intelligent, Playful

The Maine Coon is one of the largest domesticated cat breeds, renowned for its friendly and sociable nature. These cats are affectionate, intelligent, and playful, often described as "gentle giants." Maine Coons are known for their thick, water-resistant coats, tufted ears, and bushy tails. Despite their size, they are gentle and easygoing, making them excellent companions for families with children and other pets. Maine Coons are also very intelligent and can be trained to perform tricks or walk on a leash. They enjoy playing and staying active, which makes them an entertaining addition to any household.

Did You Know? Maine Coons are often believed to have been brought to North America by early seafarers, and they are the official state cat of Maine!

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-15 years
