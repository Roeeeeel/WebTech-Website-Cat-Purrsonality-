Chausie

Personalities: Energetic, Loyal, Adventurous

The Chausie is an exotic-looking cat breed that was developed by crossing domestic cats with wild jungle cats. Known for its athletic build and wild appearance, the Chausie is highly energetic and loves exploring its surroundings. Despite their wild origins, they are incredibly loyal and form strong bonds with their families. Chausies are intelligent, curious, and require plenty of stimulation to stay happy.
