Australian Mist

Personalities: Affectionate, Gentle, Playful

The Australian Mist is a friendly and sociable breed, known for its soft, misted coat and large, expressive eyes. Bred to be an indoor companion, this cat is playful, gentle, and loves being with its family. Its calm demeanor makes it a great choice for homes with children or other pets.

Did You Know? The Australian Mist was originally called the "Spotted Mist" before its name was changed to reflect its broader coat patterns.

Favorite Food: Fish and turkey

Age: Typically 15-18 years
