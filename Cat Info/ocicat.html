Ocicat

Personalities: Active, Playful, Friendly

The Ocicat is a striking breed that combines the beauty of a wild-looking spotted coat with the friendly and outgoing personality of a domestic cat. This breed is highly social, intelligent, and loves to be the center of attention. Ocicats are playful and energetic, often enjoying games like fetch and interactive play. Despite their wild appearance, they are very affectionate with their families and get along well with other pets and children.

Did You Know? The Ocicat is not a hybrid of wild cats, but rather a breed developed from domestic cats, specifically crossing Siamese, Abyssinian, and American Shorthair cats to achieve their spotted coat pattern.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-15 years
