Pixiebob

Personalities: Playful, Loyal, Intelligent

The Pixiebob is a medium-to-large breed known for its wild appearance, resembling a bobcat, but it has a calm and friendly personality. With its short or long coat and distinctive bobtail, Pixiebobs are playful, highly intelligent, and often get along well with children and other pets. They are loyal and enjoy being involved in family activities, making them great companions. Their intelligence makes them easy to train, and they love interactive play.

Did You Know? The Pixiebob was created in the 1980s and is believed to be one of the few domestic cat breeds that resemble wild bobcats.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 13-16 years
