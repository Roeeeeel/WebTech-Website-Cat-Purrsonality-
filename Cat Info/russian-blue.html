Russian Blue

Personalities: Gentle, Intelligent, Reserved

The Russian Blue is a striking breed known for its dense, plush, bluish-gray coat and vivid green eyes. These cats are highly intelligent and loyal to their families, forming strong bonds with their human companions. While they can be shy around strangers, they are affectionate and playful in familiar surroundings. The Russian Blue is also known for its quiet nature and elegant demeanor.

Did You Know? The Russian Blue is believed to have originated in Russia and was once a favorite among Russian royalty for its luxurious coat.

Favorite Food: Salmon and lamb

Age: Typically 15-20 years
