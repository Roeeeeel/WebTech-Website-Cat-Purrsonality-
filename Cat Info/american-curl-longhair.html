American Curl Longhair

Personalities: Gentle, Curious, Playful

The American Curl Longhair is a charming breed recognized for its curled-back ears and luxurious semi-long coat. These cats are affectionate and gentle, making them perfect companions for families. They retain their kitten-like playfulness well into adulthood and love to explore their surroundings.

Did You Know? The unique curl of their ears does not affect their hearing, making them just as attentive and alert as other breeds.

Favorite Food: Chicken and tuna

Age: Typically 12-16 years
