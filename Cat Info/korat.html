Korat

Personalities: Playful, Intelligent, Affectionate

The Korat is a striking breed known for its beautiful silver-blue coat and large, expressive eyes. This breed is highly intelligent, affectionate, and loyal, often forming strong bonds with its human companions. Korats are playful and curious, enjoying interactive play and exploration. They are known for their calm demeanor but are also highly vocal when they want attention. Despite their sometimes reserved nature around strangers, they are affectionate and enjoy spending time with their family members. Korats are particularly known for their loyalty and are often described as "dog-like" in their devotion.

Did You Know? The Korat is one of the oldest naturally occurring cat breeds and is considered a symbol of good luck in Thailand.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 15-20 years
