Persian

Personalities: Calm, Affectionate, Quiet

The Persian cat is known for its luxurious long fur, round face, and calm demeanor. Persians are often described as "lap cats" because of their affectionate nature and desire to be close to their human companions. They are quiet and gentle, preferring a relaxed environment where they can enjoy their time with family. Due to their luxurious coats, they do require regular grooming, but they are well worth the effort due to their sweet, loving personality.

Did You Know? The Persian breed is one of the oldest and most popular cat breeds in the world, with origins dating back to ancient Persia (modern-day Iran).

Favorite Food: Chicken and tuna

Age: Typically 12-17 years

Breed: Persian
