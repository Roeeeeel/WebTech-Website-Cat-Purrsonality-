Devon Rex

Personalities: Mischievous, Affectionate, Energetic

The Devon Rex is a quirky, playful breed known for its wavy coat, large ears, and slender body. Often referred to as the "pixie cat," the Devon Rex has a mischievous nature and thrives on human interaction. These cats are full of energy and enjoy climbing, jumping, and exploring their environment. Despite their active demeanor, they are incredibly affectionate and love to snuggle up with their owners.
