American Curl

Personalities: Curious, Friendly, Adaptable

The American Curl is a striking breed known for its unique curled-back ears. These cats are incredibly affectionate, curious, and social, making them excellent companions for families and individuals. They are playful but maintain a sweet, calm demeanor as they grow older.

Did You Know? American Curls are born with straight ears that begin to curl backward within a few days of birth.

Favorite Food: Turkey and salmon

Age: Typically 12-16 years
