Bombay

Personalities: Playful, Affectionate, Social

The Bombay cat is a sleek, black-coated breed often described as a "miniature panther." With its shiny coat and bright, copper-colored eyes, the Bombay exudes elegance and charm. These cats are affectionate, love attention, and enjoy being the center of family life. They thrive in social households and are friendly with both children and other pets.

Did You Know? The Bombay was bred to resemble a panther and is often nicknamed the "velvet panther" due to its striking appearance.

Favorite Food: Chicken and tuna

Age: Typically 12-16 years

Breed: Bombay
