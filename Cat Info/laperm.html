LaPerm

Personalities: Friendly, Affectionate, Playful

The LaPerm is a distinctive breed known for its curly, soft coat and friendly personality. These cats are affectionate, playful, and intelligent, making them excellent companions for families, children, and other pets. They are known to form strong bonds with their human companions, often following them around the house and seeking attention. LaPerms are also very social and love interactive play, making them highly entertaining. Their unique curls and expressive personalities make them stand out in any household.

Did You Know? The LaPerm breed was discovered in Oregon in the 1980s when a spontaneous genetic mutation led to the curly fur that defines the breed.

Favorite Food: Chicken and tuna

Age: Typically 12-16 years
