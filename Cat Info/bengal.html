Bengal Cat

Personalities: Energetic, Intelligent, Curious

The Bengal Cat is a stunning breed, famous for its wild, leopard-like appearance and luxurious spotted or marbled coat. These cats are highly active, playful, and intelligent, making them an ideal choice for families who can keep up with their energetic personality. Bengals thrive in interactive and stimulating environments.

Did You Know? The Bengal Cat is a hybrid breed developed by crossing domestic cats with the Asian leopard cat, giving them their striking appearance.

Favorite Food: Tuna and chicken

Age: Typically 12-16 years

Breed: Bengal
