British Shorthair

Personalities: Calm, Easygoing, Loyal

The British Shorthair is a round-faced, compact breed known for its muscular build and dense, plush coat. It is one of the oldest and most beloved cat breeds, prized for its gentle and calm nature. British Shorthairs are independent yet affectionate, and they form strong bonds with their families. These cats are quiet, low-maintenance, and tend to get along well with children and other pets.

Did You Know? The British Shorthair is one of the most iconic breeds in the UK and was famously featured in the "Alice in Wonderland" book as the Cheshire Cat.

Favorite Food: Beef and turkey

Age: Typically 12-20 years
