Birman

Personalities: Affectionate, Gentle, Loyal

The Birman, often referred to as the "Sacred Cat of Burma," is a stunning breed known for its striking blue eyes, creamy coat, and white-gloved paws. These cats are affectionate and gentle, making them perfect companions for families and individuals alike. Birmans are loyal to their owners and love being part of daily activities.

Did You Know? According to legend, the Birman cat's unique appearance was a gift from a goddess, transforming them to match the beauty of a sacred temple.

Favorite Food: Chicken and beef

Age: Typically 12-16 years

Breed: Birman
