Oriental Shorthair

Personalities: Active, Intelligent, Social

The Oriental Shorthair is a sleek and elegant breed known for its large ears, almond-shaped eyes, and variety of color patterns. They are highly intelligent and active cats, known for their curiosity and ability to learn new tricks easily. Oriental Shorthairs are very social and tend to form strong bonds with their human companions. They enjoy being around people and other pets, and they often engage in playful behavior and love interactive activities.

Did You Know? The Oriental Shorthair is a close relative to the Siamese breed and was developed to create a more diverse range of colors and patterns.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-15 years
