Norwegian Forest

Personalities: Affectionate, Independent, Playful

The Norwegian Forest cat is a majestic breed known for its thick, water-repellent coat and bushy tail. These cats are independent but affectionate, often forming strong bonds with their human families. Originally from the forests of Norway, they have adapted to cold climates and are excellent climbers and hunters. Norwegian Forest cats are playful and curious, enjoying both interactive play and quiet time. They are well-suited to active households and can coexist well with other pets.
