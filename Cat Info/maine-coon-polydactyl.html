Maine Coon Polydactyl

Personalities: Friendly, Intelligent, Playful

The Maine Coon Polydactyl is a special variation of the Maine Coon breed known for its extra toes. Polydactyls are distinguished by their unique paw structure, where they may have one or more extra toes on each paw. Maine Coon Polydactyls maintain all the wonderful characteristics of the Maine Coon breed, including their friendly and social nature, intelligence, and playful demeanor. They are gentle giants, often growing to large sizes, and are known for their affectionate and loyal personalities. These cats are also highly adaptable, making them great companions for families, children, and other pets.

Did You Know? The Maine Coon Polydactyl was historically prized by sailors, as the extra toes helped the cat climb rigging and catch mice aboard ships.

Favorite Food: Chicken and salmon

Age: Typically 12-15 years

Breed: Maine Coon Polydactyl
