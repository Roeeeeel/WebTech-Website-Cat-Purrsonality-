LaPerm Shorthair

Personalities: Friendly, Curious, Playful

The LaPerm Shorthair is a charming and affectionate breed known for its soft, curly coat and sweet personality. These cats are friendly, curious, and playful, making them excellent companions for families, children, and other pets. The LaPerm Shorthair's coat, while shorter than the longhaired variety, still retains the distinctive curls that make them so unique. They are intelligent and love interactive play, often seeking attention and cuddles from their human companions. Their laid-back yet curious nature makes them easygoing and adaptable to various environments.

Did You Know? The LaPerm breed originated in Oregon in the 1980s and is believed to have been caused by a spontaneous genetic mutation, leading to their signature curly fur.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 12-16 years
