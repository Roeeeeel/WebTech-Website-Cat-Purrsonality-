Turkish Angora

Personalities: Energetic, Intelligent, Affectionate

The Turkish Angora is a graceful and elegant breed known for its long, silky coat and striking eyes. These cats are highly energetic and playful, often described as "mischievous" because of their love for exploration and active play. Turkish Angoras are also incredibly intelligent and can easily learn new tricks and tasks. Despite their independent nature, they are affectionate and form strong bonds with their families. They tend to be very social and often enjoy interacting with both people and other pets. Their agility and speed make them excellent jumpers and climbers.

Did You Know? The Turkish Angora is one of the oldest and most natural breeds in the world, originating from Turkey, where it is considered a national treasure.

Favorite Food: Fish and chicken

Age: Typically 12-15 years
