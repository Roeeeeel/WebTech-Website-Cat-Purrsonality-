American Bobtail Shorthair

Personalities: Affectionate, Curious, Independent

The American Bobtail Shorthair is a unique variation of the American Bobtail breed, characterized by its short coat and trademark bobbed tail. These cats are independent yet loving, making them an excellent choice for families and single owners alike. Their intelligence and curiosity add to their charming personality.

Did You Know? Despite their wild appearance, American Bobtail Shorthairs are known for their gentle and adaptable nature.

Favorite Food: Salmon and chicken

Age: Typically 12-15 years

Breed: American Bobtail Shorthair
