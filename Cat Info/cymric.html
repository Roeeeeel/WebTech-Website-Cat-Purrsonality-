Cymric

Personalities: Loyal, Playful, Gentle

The Cymric is a long-haired version of the Manx cat, known for its tailless appearance and dense, fluffy coat. These cats are incredibly loyal and thrive in close relationships with their families. They are playful and intelligent, often enjoying games like fetch or learning tricks. With their gentle disposition, Cymrics get along well with children and other pets, making them a wonderful addition to any home.

Did You Know? The Cymric's lack of a tail is caused by a natural genetic mutation, which is a hallmark trait of the breed.

Favorite Food: Salmon and chicken

Age: Typically 12-16 years
