Burmilla

Personalities: Affectionate, Playful, Intelligent

The Burmilla is an elegant breed with a short, shiny coat and an expressive face. It is a cross between a Burmese and a Chinchilla Persian, which gives it a unique and striking appearance. Known for their friendly and affectionate nature, Burmillas are highly social, intelligent, and enjoy the company of their family. They can be playful and curious but are also content to curl up for a quiet nap.

Did You Know? The Burmilla breed was developed by accident when a Burmese cat was bred with a Chinchilla Persian, resulting in the stunning silver-coated Burmilla.

Favorite Food: Fish and chicken

Age: Typically 12-16 years
