Burmese

Personalities: Affectionate, Playful, Curious

The Burmese cat is known for its sleek, shiny coat and large, expressive eyes. These cats are often described as "dog-like" due to their loyal and affectionate nature. They are highly social, enjoy attention, and often form strong bonds with their human families. Burmese cats are playful, energetic, and enjoy being in the company of other pets.

Did You Know? Burmese cats are known for their unique "puppy-like" personality, often following their owners around and even playing fetch.

Favorite Food: Chicken and fish

Age: Typically 10-16 years

Breed: Burmese
